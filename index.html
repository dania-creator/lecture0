Introduction

Blind people have been suffuring from going out alone , they always need an assiatant to assist them or to lead them from falling in streets ,in this project we are going to help the blind person to go out easily without assistant ,a low-cost stick is designed for blind people that enables them to walk without any assistance.

Methodology

This can be done by using a sensor that detects obstacles from a specific range and alaret the blind person by a buzzer "meaning that there is an obstacle infront of you" , i have used an Ultra sonic sensor for detecting obstacles from a range of 30m to 40m and used a buzzer to give sound when there is obstacle.

Conclusion

we have connected the Ultrasonic sensor and the buzzer with the arduino and programmed it to give the blind person sound when obsctacle is 30cm close.
